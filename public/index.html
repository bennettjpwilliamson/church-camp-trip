The Area

Silver Creek & the Plunge

The campground is in the Boise National Forest, about 4,490 feet up, right on Silver Creek — around two hours north of Boise. A short walk from camp is Silver Creek Plunge, a family-run place with a warm geothermal pool, a small store, and rental cabins.

There's no shortage of things to do if you want them, or you can just sit by the fire.

  • Geothermal pool — spring-fed and around 100°F. Day passes are $10/adult and $8/kid, paid at the Plunge office.
  • Hiking — Peace Creek, Silver Creek Summit, and Bull Creek trails start nearby.
  • Fishing — Silver Creek is stocked with rainbow trout. You'll need a pole and an Idaho fishing license.
  • Store — snacks, ice, firewood, propane, and towel rentals at the Plunge.
  • Stargazing — no cell service means no light pollution either. The Milky Way is easy to see.

Property map

Our group site sits east of the bridge, just north of the volleyball field. The pool, store, and parking are a short walk south.
